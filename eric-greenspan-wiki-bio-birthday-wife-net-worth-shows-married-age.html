Eric Greenspan: Wiki, Bio, Birthday, Wife, Net Worth, Shows, Married, Age and details: Eric is a famous celebrity chef. He specializes in the field of processed food especially processed cheese dishes. He is popular by the name King of Comfort Food among the gourmets. Eric was labeled as one of the eight Hot Chefs by the Angeleno Magazines.

Eric Greenspan Life and Education

Greenspan is a 46-year-old cooking expert. He was born on the 18th of April in the year 1975. He was born in New Jersey but was raised in California, United States. Eric spent his adulthood in Fullerton. As he was born and raised in the United States, Eric holds an American nationality. He belongs to multiracial ethnicity.

Talking about his educational qualifications, Eric has done his high schooling at a local school in Calabasas. For his  Bachelor’s degree, Eric attended UC Berkeley’s Haas School of Business. He earned his degree in Business Administration. Later, he urged his parents to send him to Paris. He attended Le Cordon Bleu Culinary School.

Eric Greenspan Career

Eric has started his career from practically nothing. He used to wash dishes when he was in college. After a while, he got the job of head cook at a local breakfast joint. He has worked for a lot of people and restaurants when he was in New York. After the 9/11 attacks, he relocated to Los Angeles. Eric opened his first restaurant The Foundry in the year 2007. It received amazing reviews and was nominated for various awards and accolades.

At the present, Eric is the owner of several restaurants. He is the owner of The Foundry, Greenspan’s Grilled Cheese, and The Roof of Wilshire. Apart from cooking, he has also written a few cookbooks that might help aspiring chefs.

Eric Greenspan Personal Life

The celebrity chef is married to his wife Jamie Molever. They were dating long back before their marriage and finally exchanged the wedding vows in the year 2012. The couple has celebrated their 8th wedding anniversary last year in 2020. From their wedding, the couple has two sons and they are the apple of their eyes.
