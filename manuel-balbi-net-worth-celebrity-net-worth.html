What is Manuel Balbi's Net Worth?

Manuel Balbi is a Mexican actor who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Manuel Balbi was born in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico in March 1978. He studied acting in San Luis Potosi at the theater workshop of the Mexican Social Security Institute. He moved to Mexico City at 18 and was accepted into Televisa's Center for Artistic Education. His first acting role acme in the television series Siempre te amare in 2000. He starred in the TV series Friends and Rivals in 2001 and Tal para cual and Agua y aceite in 2002. In 2004 he starred as Mirko in the series Gitanas and in 2005 as Nicolas in Decisiones.

He also starred in the TV series La Viuda de Blanco in 2006 and Deseo Prohibido in 2008. He starred in the television series Vuelveme a querer in 2009 and Las Aparicio in 2010. The same year he starred in his first feature films Seres: Genesis and By Day and by Night. In 2011 Balbi starred in the TV series Morir en Martes 2 and in 2012 he starred in the series Rosa Diamante. He starred in the movie Chiapas the Heart of Coffee in 2012 and then in the TV series La Patrona and Fortuna in 2013. In 2014 Manuel starred in the TV series El Mariachi and El Senor de los Cielos as well as the movie Casi treinta.
