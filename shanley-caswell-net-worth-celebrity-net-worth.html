What is Shanley Caswell's Net Worth?

Shanley Caswell is an American actress who has a net worth of $400 thousand dollars. Shanley Caswell was born in Sarasota, Florida in December 1991. Shanley stared acting at eight years old in local theater. In high school she was involved with a theater troupe who educated teenagers on social issues.
