What is Natalia Oreiro's Net Worth?

Natalia Oreiro is a Uruguayan singer, actress, and fashion designer who has a net worth of $10 million. Natalia Oreiro was born in Montevideo, Uruguay in May 1977. She started studying drama at eight years old and appeared in over 30 television commercials during her teens including ones for Pepsi, Coca Cola, and Johnson & Johnson. Oreiro moved to Argentina at 16 and worked as an MTV VJ. She made her television debut in 1994 in the TV series Alta comeida, To Learn to Fly, and Unconquerable Heart. From 1998 to 1999 Oreiro starred in the television series Wild Angel. She starred in the TV series Runaway Lady in 2002 and in the series The Desire in 2004.

From 2006 to 2007 she starred in the television series You Are the One. Natalia starred as the title character in the series Amanda O from 2008 to 2009. She starred as Isabel Reyes in the series Lynch from 2012 to 2013. Oreiro has also starred in the movies Cleopatra, Las vidas posibles, La peli, Musica en espera, Miss Tacuarembo, My First Wedding, Clandestine Childhood, and The German Doctor. In 2012 Natalia Oreiro won a Best Actress Award from the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences of Argentina. As a singer Oreiro has released three studio albums.
