Celebrity kids are just the cutest, but Angelina Jolie‘s teenage daughter Zahara Jolie-Pitt might not want to be called cute anymore because when the 14-year-old stepped out on Sunday, Jan. 13 with her famous mom during a luxurious shopping trip in LA, she looked so grown-up!

It seems like it was just yesterday that Zahara was a little girl. Angelina, 43, previously adopted her first daughter from Ethiopia in July 2005 and Zahara was later adopted by her dad and Angie’s now ex-husband, Brad Pitt, a year later in 2006. Years after she joined the Jolie-Pitt family, a woman who claimed to be Zahara’s birth mother tried to contact her famous daughter.

“I just want her to know that I am alive and here and long to be able to speak with her. I do not want my daughter back but just to be in contact with her,” the woman told the Daily Mail in 2017 with the help of an interpreter. “Angelina has been more of a mother to her than I have ever been. She has been with her since she was a baby, but that does not mean I do not miss her. I miss her all the time.”

Angelina and Brad, 55 — who split after two years of marriage in 2016 — are also parents to five other kids: Maddox Jolie-Pitt, 17, Pax Jolie-Pitt, 15, Shiloh Jolie-Pitt, 12, and twins Knox and Vivienne Jolie-Pitt, both 10.
