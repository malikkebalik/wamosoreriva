Polaris Scrambler 500 Price, Specs and Review 2022

Polaris Scrambler 500 featured a 499 cubic centimeters displacement, 10:8:1 compression ratio, 3.5 gallons/13.2 liters Fuel tank capacity, single-lever hydraulic front discs, and rear hydraulic disc foot brake.

About Polaris

Polaris Inc. is an American manufacturer of motorcycles, snowmobiles, ATVs, and neighborhood electric vehicles founded in 1954; 67 years ago by Edgar Hetteen, Allen Hetteen, and David Johnson.

Polaris Headquarters is in Medina, Minnesota, the U.S. and covers the whole Worldwide area (except Afghanistan, Cuba, Iran, Syria, and North Korea).

Polaris offers Snowmobiles, All-terrain vehicles, Motorcycles, Moto-Roadsters, Boats, Commercial vehicles, Ultra-Light Tactical Vehicles, and Parts, Garments, and Accessories.
