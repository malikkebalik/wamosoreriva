Sophie Cookson Bra Size, Age, Weight, Height, Measurements

Biography:

Sophie Cookson was born on 15 May 1990 in England. She trained at the Oxford School of Drama. In 2013, she started acting professional as she made her first screen appearance on Moonfleet, a miniseries. She next appeared on the TV series Rosamunde Pilcher and the TV film Unknown Heart.

She then got her break after she landed the role of Roxy Morton in Kingsman: The Secret Service starring Colin Firth. She next appeared in the Hollywood feature The Huntsman: Winter’s War. In 2016, she acted in the films The Crucifixion and Ashes in the Show. She’s currently filming the upcoming Kingsman: The Golden Circle.
