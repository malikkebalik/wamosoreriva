What is Ric Savage's Net Worth?

Ric Savage is an American television personality and former professional wrestler who has a net worth of $3 million. Ric Savage was born in Sylva, North Carolina in June 1969. His ring name was "Heavy Metal" Ric Savage. He was trained by Ivan Koloff and Chuck Justice and wrestled from 1991 to 1997 in the Professional Wrestling Federation, World Championship Wrestling, United States Wrestling Alliance, National Wrestling Alliance, Extreme Championship Wrestling, National Championship Wrestling, and more.

Savage became the host of the reality television series Savage Family Diggers on Spike TV in 2013. He was also featured on the series Paranormal After Party from 2014 to 2016. He conducts haunted ghost tours and has hosted the Savage-Turner Rock Express Radio Show/Podcast with Warrant's Erik Turner. As a wrestler Savage held the GAWF Southern Heavyweight Championship, IWA United States Heavyweight Championship, NCW World Tag Team Championship, SWA World Heavyweight Championship, and WCW Southeastern Regional Six-Man Tag Team Championship.
