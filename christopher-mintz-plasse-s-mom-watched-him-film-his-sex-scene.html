Christopher Mintz-Plasse's Mom Watched Him Film His Sex Scene

Movies

In the movie 'Superbad,' Christopher Mintz-Plasse's mom was on set while he filmed his sex scene.

Published on February 2, 2021

2 min read

Superbad stars Jonah Hill and Michael Cera and was written by Seth Rogen and Evan Goldberg. The 2007 movie is loosely based on Rogen and Goldberg’s high school experience, and the two began working on the screenplay as teenagers.

Christopher Mintz-Plasse appears in Superbad as Fogell, a breakout character widely known as “McLovin.” In an interview with The Guardian, Mintz-Plasse revealed that his mom was present on the set of Superbad when he filmed a sex scene.

Christopher Mintz-Plasse did not have acting experience before ‘Superbad’

Superbad premiered in 2007, and the coming-of-age movie is considered a classic nowadays. The R-rated film follows two characters named Seth (Hill) and Evan (Cera) during their senior year of high school.

Mintz-Plasse plays their classmate Fogell who gets a fake ID during the movie with the name “McLovin.” Superbad was Mintz-Plasse’s first movie, and he was chosen for the role because he was able to have unbridled confidence while also playing a high school nerd.

“You could tell he was a kid who had probably seen the inside of a locker,” casting director Allison Jones told The New Yorker in 2015.

The actor’s mom was present on the ‘Superbad’ set

Not only was Superbad his first movie, but Mintz-Plasse was also only 17 years old when he played Fogell. A couple of years after the movie premiered, Mintz-Plasse sat down for an interview with The Guardian.

In the interview, Mintz-Plasse revealed that his mom was on set when he filmed a sex scene for Superbad, saying:

“My first ever sex scene in a movie was in Superbad. Because I was 17, for legal reasons my mother had to be on the set. It was real awkward but it worked out OK because when I watched the movie with her the sex scene wasn’t awkward because she’d been right there when it happened. Afterwards we didn’t talk about it; we still don’t speak about that moment. Now, I’m 19 so my mom doesn’t have to be in the room when I have sex, thank God.”

Jonah Hill did not like Christopher Mintz-Plasse at first

In Superbad, Fogell and Seth repeatedly clash due to their friendship with Evan. Seth finds himself jealous of Fogell because Fogell and Evan plan to room together at college. This rivalry partially continued offscreen as Hill did not enjoy working with Mintz-Plasse.

“Chris just immediately shut me down,” Hill told The Ringer. “So combative. I was really annoyed because this guy wouldn’t let me say anything. And I told Seth and Evan that and they were like, ‘He’s perfect.'”

Rogen agreed, saying, “Jonah f*cking hated him… He was all over Jonah’s lines, completely disrespectful of the process, probably due to a lack of experience. I remember he walked out of the room and Jonah was like, ‘Not that guy.’ And we were just like, ‘Oh my god, you don’t understand how much more that makes us want him.'”
