The 4 Best Kitchen Trash Cans in 2023

When you buy through our links, Insider may earn an affiliate commission. Learn more

A trash can is one of the home essentials, you may not think to put a lot of thought into, but trust us, the right garbage receptacle can make or break your kitchen experience. To help you find the best trash can for your kitchen, we spoke with organization experts, cleaning professionals, and a bartender with decades of experience. They all agreed that a standalone unit with a tightly sealing lid and a trusty step mechanism is best for most people, and one with a sensor or push-button is best for those who want a more accessible option. However, under-counter units, slimline bins, and sensor-operated cans can all be good options depending on your space and accessibility needs.

We tried five different garbage cans (and considered many more at retail stores) to determine the best ones. We tested them for sturdiness and rigidity, ease of opening and general use, and, when applicable, the effectiveness of a lid at both creating an even and reliable seal and containing smells (within reason). Our top choice is the SimpleHuman Step Trash Can; though pricey, it has an attractive appearance, is available in many sizes and shapes, and is the most sanitary and reliable option we've come across.  For a budget-friendly pick, the 13-gallon Rubbermaid Step-On Garbage Can is no-frills but heavy-duty and built to last.
