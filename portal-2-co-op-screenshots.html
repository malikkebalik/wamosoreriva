Portal 2 Co-Op Screenshots

Recommended Videos

Yesterday Valve put up a brand new gallery of screenshots from the upcoming sci-fi puzzler Portal 2, showcasing the highly anticipated co-op mode where you and a buddy can tackle all new Portal-ed puzzles together…expect lots of arguing and laughing. Valve was recently proud to announce that PS3 and PC players will be able to play with each other (stop laughing you) across the platforms and team up. They’ve also teased about support for the PC peripheral ‘Razor Hydra’, hinting at some special motion control levels.

Portal 2 is boasting a whole arsenal of new tricks and twists for players to scratch their heads over, and will be hitting stores on April 21st. In the meantime though, check out the new pics and try not to grin too much at the (previously released) co-op trailer.
