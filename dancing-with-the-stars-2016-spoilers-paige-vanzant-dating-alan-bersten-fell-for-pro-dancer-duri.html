Dancing With The Stars 2016 Spoilers: Paige VanZant Dating Alan Bersten Fell For Pro Dancer Duri

Dancing With The Stars 2016 spoilers tease that there is a new couple on the ABC dancing reality TV show – and it is not who you think it may be. Recently, fans were shocked when Mischa Barton and her pro partner, Artem Chigvintsev, were caught on a date. But, according to a new report, Mischa Barton is not the only celeb who found love on Season 22 of Dancing With The Stars.

According to a new report, Paige VanZant is dating one of the Dancing With The Stars pros, and it’s not her partner Mark Ballas (he’s happily engaged). Remember at the beginning of Season 22 when Mark Ballas was injured and had to sit out one of his and Paige’s dances, and Alan Bersten filled in? Well, word on the set of DWTS is that Paige and Alan totally hit it off, and they have been secretly seeing each other ever since! If you recall, Paige VanZant and Alan Bersten had AMAZING chemistry when they took the DWTS stage.

An inside source dished to Entertainment Tonight that Paige and Alan are definitely more than just friends – but they are trying to keep their relationship off the radar for now. The insider told ET, “They just got to know one another during the show and rehearsal and they took a liking to one another. They aren’t really showing it off, but they are definitely interested in one another.”

Paige VanZant won’t be the first celeb to fall for her Dancing With The Stars partner – and she most likely won’t be the last. So DWTS fans, what do you think of the new couple?
