Anwar Jibawi

Who is Anwar Jibawi?

Anwar Jibawi is an online celebrity, actor, and director. He was born in 1991 in Chicago and grew up in California. The star celebrates his birthday on August 9. Anwar is mainly famous for his comedic talent. He often works with many popular content creators and artists.

Who are Anwar Jibawi's parents?

The star's parents moved from Pakistan to the USA in search of a more prosperous life for their family. His father's name is Dais Salama. Anwar's mother Amal sometimes appears in his videos. He has five siblings.

YouTube career

In 2013, the comedian created a Vine account and managed to build a large following. Three years later, he started his YouTube channel and became a member of Shots Studios, which is a management and production company. It creates online comedy and music videos and manages digital artists.

In 2017, Jibawi helped Mike Tyson start a YouTube channel, and later they often appeared in each other's videos. The comedian created content together with such famous actor as Marlon Wayans. Also, he collaborates with King Bach and Jerry Purpdrank.

In 2018, Anwar directed a music video for Aitana. It gained over 30 million views on the platform. As of March 2022, he has 7,05 million followers. According to the description on his channel, he uploads new content every week.

Who's Anwar Jibawi's girlfriend?

Many of Jibawi's comedy sketches revolve around the girlfriend topic. He collaborates on videos with Hannah Stocking, Lele Pons, and others. However, he has never officially confirmed being in a relationship.

How much is Anwar Jibawi's net worth?

Apart from acting and making viral videos, the star appeared in commercials and fashion week presentations. As of 2022, his approximate net worth is around $4 - 5 million.
