Tracker Season 1, Episode 10 Spoilers: Diving Into The Wild (Photos, Promo)

As Season 1 of Tracker rolls on, fans continue to stay glued to their primetime television sets. This first season has been an explosive one so far, and the next show, scheduled for this coming Sunday, likely won’t disappoint.

Tracker Season 1, Episode 10 Spoilers: The Hunter For Missing Children

Tracker Season 1, Episode 10 spoilers note next Sunday’s show is titled, “Into The Wild”. Added teasers suggest that Colter Shaw (Justin Hartley) will head into Idaho’s wilderness to find the missing adult children for an aerial outfitting company’s owner, who also happens to be a friend of Reenie Greene’s (Fiona Rene) dad. The kids were last seen in a plane that took off amidst bad weather, while a mysterious client was also on board.

Will Colter get to the bottom of things and what happens next? As this does have to do with a father and his missing children, will the show also continue to explore Colter’s own family issues within this episode and offer additional insight into his history? Fans will have to tune in to see!

Tracker Season 1, Episode 10 Spoilers: Building Up To The Season Finale

There are no more hiatuses for Tracker’s Season 1 and with the finale mere weeks away, momentum will be building as the show wraps up before the summer hits. It’s nothing but new content from here on out, so fans should expect some questions to be answered along the way, but also plenty of other questions to pop up, along with some surprises, and of course, drama!

Episode 10 of Tracker is slated for Sunday, April 28, at 9 p.m. ET, while the season finale for the show is scheduled to place on Sunday, May 19 at 9 p.m. ET.
