Exclusive: Brielle Biermann Says That Kim Zolciak Will Be the 'Best' Grandma

Credit: Kris Connor/Getty Images for Sherri Hill

Oh baby! Brielle Biermann spilled to us about having kids and how she feels about her mom Kim Zolciak becoming a grandma.

Baby fever! Brielle Biermann opened up about having kids in an exclusive statement to Celebuzz.

The 26-year-old also discussed her mom Kim Zolciak becoming a grandma, something that they all are evidently excited about.

In fact, when Brielle appeared on Behind the Velvet Rope with David Yontef in October of 2022, she revealed that Kim had been begging her to get pregnant.

“She wants me to have a child,” she said, according to Page Six. “She’s like, ‘Come on, you’re not getting any younger. Like, I want a little baby. And if you don’t have it, I’m going to have one.’”

Evidently, The Real Housewives of Atlanta star would have also been fine with a pregnancy from her younger daughter Ariana Biermann.

Brielle described, “She’s like, ‘Ariana come on. Like, one of y’all have a baby. I’ll take care of it.’ I’m like, ‘You’re insane. I’m not having kids.’”

Although children were not in her plans, the influencer was clear that it would be “the happiest day” of her mother’s life if she were to get pregnant.

“Oh my God, she would love it. She would lose her mind,” the Don’t Be Tardy star detailed. “[She] would be so excited that there was a baby coming around.”

Brielle explained that her mother loves babies and always has baby fever, but she knows that she wouldn’t want to deal with another child in the house.

“I always used to say [to my mom] growing up, ‘I want you to raise my kids because you did such a good job,’” she recalled. The model also praised her mother and father at the time for having a house where she could “share everything you could think of with them.”

It’s unclear if Brielle’s feelings towards her adopted dad have changed since Kim’s messy split from Kroy Biermann, but she has confirmed that she looks forward to having her mom’s help raising a baby.
