What is Shane Bunting's Net Worth?

Shane Bunting is a Canadian rapper who has a net worth of $1 million. Shane Bunting was born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada in October 1975. He is best known by his stage name Madchild and is a part of the hip hop group Swollen Members. Bunting released his debut studio album Dope Sick in 2012. He has also released the albums Lawn Mower Man in 2013 and Silver Tongue Devil in 2015. Madchild has also released six EPs and a mixtape.

In 2011 he was banned from entering the United States due to ties to the Hells Angels Motorcycle Club. Swollen Members have released nine studio albums starting with their debut Balance in 1999. Their 2013 album Beautiful Death Machine reached #3 in Canada and #3 on the US Heatseekers chart. The group has won four Juno Awards and five MuchMusic Video Awards. Bunting took his stage name from a member of the Rock Steady Crew. He has been involved in rap battles and battled Daylyt in 2015.
