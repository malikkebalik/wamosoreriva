What Does The Name DDG Stand For?

Darryl Dwayne Granberry Jr. is an American rapper, singer, and YouTuber better known publicly with the stage names PontiacMadeDDG and simply DDG. After graduating from high school in 2015, DDG began making videos and increased his content with YouTube vlogs while studying at Central Michigan University. A year later, he stopped attending college to concentrate on his music and YouTube careers.

He has his own record label called Zooted Music, and Eric O’Connor and Dimitri Hurt have been his longtime managers in 2020 when his record was established. But before that, he signed to Epic Records in 2018. More than 200 million people have listened to his smash song, which features rapper Blueface and is called Moonwalking in Calabasas. DDG is a valedictorian of his class during his days at the International Tech Academy school.
