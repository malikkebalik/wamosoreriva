What is Hannah Gadsby's Net Worth?

Hannah Gadsby is an Australian comedian, writer, actor, and television personality who has a net worth of $1 million. Hannah Gadsby was born in Smithton, Tasmania, Australia in January 1978. She won the Raw Comedy competition in 2006. From 2014 to 2016 she starred as Hannah on the television series Please Like Me. Gadsby has had recurring roles on the TV series The Librarians as Carmel from 2009 to 2010 and Underbelly as Charlie in 2013.

She has written for the TV series Adam Hills in Gordon St Tonight, Hannah Gadsby's Oz, and Please Like Me. She has appeared in the comedy specials Hannah Gadsby: Nanette, Hannah Gadsby's Nakedy Nudes, Hannah Gadsby: Renaissance Woman, and more. She has appeared on television in Australia and New Zealand and on British radio. Hannah Gadsby is openly lesbian and has ADHD and autism. Her show Douglas toured the US and Australia in 2019.
